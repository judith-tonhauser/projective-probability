You will read a fact about the world and are asked to assess how likely an event is, given that fact. For example:

Fact: Patty lives in Canada.

How likely is it that Patty watches hockey?

You will give your answer on a sliding scale from 'impossible' to 'definitely'.

There are a total of 22 items. The task will take about 4 minutes and you will be paid 55 cents.

We have included some items for which we already know the answer. If you are paying attention, you are sure to get those right! At the end of the task, we will ask you four short questions about you and your language use. You will be paid no matter what you answer there, so please answer truthfully.

Thank you!
